In message <003101c1fe87$45a277c0$0600a8c0@sk.sympatico.ca>, "Cory Cone" writes:
>I was wondering if anyone knows a way with Xitami to limit the bandwidth 
>a site can use, you know like the monthley transfer rate could be set to 
>1GB or 500MB...Anyone know how to do this?

The Xitami 2.5 beta can "shape" the bandwidth used to limit it to a
certain rate per second.  But Xitami doesn't have a way of limiting the
bandwidth used over a longer period of time, unless you simply slow
everything down so that the most it could send out in a month would be
500MB or so.  (If you did that it'd always be very slow...)
