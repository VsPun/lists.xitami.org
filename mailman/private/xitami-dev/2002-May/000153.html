Hi!

Here's my setup:
OS:  Win98
Xitami path:  c:\xitami
JDK path:  c:\jdk1.3.1_02
Conacom servlet runner path:  c:\xitami\java
gnujsp jar path:  c:\xitami\java\gnujsp-1.0.1\lib

I renamed the ff CMD files to BAT files for Windows compatibility:
1) runner.cmd -> runner.bat
2) restart.cmd -> restart.bat
3) stop.cmd -> stop.bat

The servlet runner .ini and .bat files, and also servlet.properties,  are placed in c:\xitami\java.

I modified runner.ini such that:
1) sessionmanagerconfig=mem_sessionmgr.ini
2) servlet_cfg=servlet.ini
3) locale=en,US

I created servlet.ini, which is patterned from sv_demo.ini.

I created servlet.properties, patterned from sv_demo.properties.

The classpath (this should all be in one line) in runner.bat and servlet.properties is:
.;/xitami/java/runner.jar;/xitami/java/javax.jar;
/xitami/java/gnujsp-1.0.1/lib/gnujsp10test.jar;
/xitami/java/gnujsp-1.0.1/lib/openxml-1.2-w3c.jar;
/xitami/java/gnujsp-1.0.1/lib/servlet-2.0-plus.jar

The conacom test servlet (net.conacom.servletrunner.util.TestServlet)  runs OK.
The gnujsp test servlet (org.gjt.jsp.test.GNUJSPTestServlet) runs OK.
The gnujsp servlet (org.gjt.jsp.JspServlet) returned an init() error, logged in srvl_20020507.log.  It appears that the settings in servlet.properties were not read, and defaults were assumed by the gnujsp servlet.

I'm attaching the config files together with this post. Please reply if you have any questions.

BTW, gnujsp can be freely downloaded in
    http://www.klomp.org/gnujsp/.

Thanks for your help!
Phil
