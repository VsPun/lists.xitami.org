Vishal,

I currently run version 2.5c0 at my workplace and also locally on my 
laptop and have never seen the error you have described.
Over the years, I have had some infrequent crashes when using Xitami, 
but generally have found it to be fairly reliable.

You do not mention if you have tried to re-install Xitami, it might be 
worthwhile to get a fresh copy and re-install as this may eliminate the 
possibility of damaged files.

I have run Xitami with Norton's Internet Security, but (from memory as 
it was a while ago) apart from having to let the Nortons know it was OK 
to  let Xitami run, I had no problems. Maybe check the firewall settings.
