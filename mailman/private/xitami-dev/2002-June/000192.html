Rafael Cano Pérez <rafael.cano@o-labs.com> wrote:

Good questions.  Let me answer each of them.

>  When will cvs.imatix.com be available again?.

Not for a while.  We have decided that CVS was not a good way to 
deliver the Xitami source code, taking too much effort to build.  We 
will provide source kits as zip/tgz files.  At a later stage we may 
decide to make daily snapshots of the development CVS.

>  How fresh is the New Zeeland site, and why have
>  the building files disappear?.

The iMatix NZ site will be absorbed into the imatix.com site.

>  How stable is SMT v 3.0 ( smtsock ) for building
>  a production service?.

It is stable and we are using it for production services.

>  How is the near future regarding SFL & SMT distribution?.

We are in the process of repackaging all our source code.  iMatix.com 
will have news once we are ready to go public.  Basically we will 
have packages for:

 - base tools (SFL, SMT, GSL, htmlpp, etc.)
 - Xitami 2.4 (including archaic SFL & SMT)
 - Xitami 2.5 (including archaic SFL & SMT)

plus Xitami 3.0 once that starts to become stable.

>  Are there plans to extend the SFL library for XML functionality, like
>  search and sort?.

Yes, we have planned to make such extensions to sflxml.  However, I 
can't tell you when.

>  How can I pay you a beer for your good work?.

Come to Brussels and visit us.  We are always happy to share a beer.
