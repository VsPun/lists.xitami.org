> Our application area is document imaging including production

> With the growing trend towards offshore processing, we now are
> faced with customers scanning on-site and then transferring
> files to an offshore server. The quantum of data could be

You really should look at some document specific compression 
methods then.

Take a look at http://www.lizardtech.com/ .

and 

http://www.djvuzone.org/ .

Using DjVu compression I can get a 43+Meg document down to under
10 Megs.  You can see these examples on 
http://www.unusualresearch.com/ .

Simple black and white page scans might be as low as 50K per 
page, from originals that are in the meg range.

There are specific compression methods for medical imaging and 
maps as well.
