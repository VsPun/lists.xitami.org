This is a problem.
You cannot specify to the ADSL modem how it should react or what IP it has 
or receives from your IP.
If you have specified to receive a static IP from your ISP then use the IP 
you where assigned.

If not, then I am afraid you are on a static IP and that is only displayed 
to your "router" part of the ADSL modem and not your PC.

What you would have to do is treat it as a dynamic IP in this case and try 
to hook up with some form of free DynIP service to get the site to show up 
properly.
