You should have port 80 forwared through your router, if you have one.
And it should be open in a software firewall too.  Also, your ISP may be
blocking port 80 from coming to you so you don't run a webserver. You
could check with your ISP if its allowed in your service agreemet.  If
its not allowed, don't ask. If port 80 is blocked by your ISP you could
change try changing it to a different port like 8080. Your uses would
then access it by using www.hopkinsfamily.com:8080
