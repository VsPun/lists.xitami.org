The 10 max concurrent users per server, is applied to MS Access databases 
accessed through ADO or ODBC through a CGI or ASP script.
This can be circumvented on all Windows machines starting from Windows 2000 
WST and up, by using a CGI calling the access db directly through ADO.
I didn't know there was such a limitation to Xitami?! Odd...

512 MB DDR-RAM, and 500 GB disk space. 250 Is allocated to Virthost dir's,
