On Wed, 19 May 2004, Ingo wrote:

> Greetings
> Is there a way to block IP addresses in Xitami? Or will I have to look for
> some type of utility? My router does not offer that function.
> Any help would be greatly appreciated.

Under the [Security] section of your *.cfg file, use
webmask=!<IP address or range>,*

i.e.

[Security]
   webmask=!211.*,*

would block 211.0.0.0/8, but let through everything else (the trailing
",*" is important).
