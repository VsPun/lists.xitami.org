I have 4 systems running Win98SE, one of them runs Xitami.

I didn't experience any issues with Xitami itself, but all of the systems
became unstable; random crashes mostly (sometimes a blue screen, sometimes
a reboot).  Turning off KB891711 fixed it on all of the machines.

It may be an interaction with something else that's not well behaved,
though it's hard to believe since the 4 systems have little in common
besides Win98.

Regardless, removing the "patch" removed the problem.

Gary
