Put it inside c:/xitami/cgi-bin and then call it
with the browser http://localhost/cgi-bin/hello.bat  and see if it works.

If the simple hello.bat which has just this one line in it:

     @echo Hello!

outputs "Hello!", then your problem will have nothing to do with 
Xitami's cgi-bin configuration.
