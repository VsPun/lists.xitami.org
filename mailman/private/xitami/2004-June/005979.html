Hi Jill,

Your sites shouldn't "cancel" each other out at all.  I host a dozen sites  
and folks can visit any of them at any time.

Are you using the virtual host capabilities of Xitami?  i.e. do you have  
separate configuration files for each of your hosts, and these files are  
referenced in your xitami.cfg (or better yet, a default.cfg)?

If you have a VERY slow Internet connection and complicated pages, it's  
possible that you're seeing a white page while Xitami delivers the goods  
to you.  Do you see a progress bar or any other indication that the site  
is still loading while you see that white page?

Michael
