Hi Ron, nothing easier than that.

I just tried after I saw your messge.
2 steps:

1. create a dir for each of your virtual domains in the error dir
like \xitami-25\app\errors\virtualdomainname

2. open up your admin interface, open your virtual domain, go to errors,
in there change the path to @errors\virtualdomainname\page
save all the way back thtough , restart and you are done.
