> The answer is plain and simple:
> Sqirrelmail is a webmail interface with which one can connect to his
> e-mail box from  anywhere over the internet. The only need is a browser,
> as long as there is an imap  mailserver where the mailbox resides on.You
> don't need a mailprogramm to check your  mail.
> And it is not a mailserver and for sure not ment to sent mail to the
> authors of  squirrelmail.
>
> The reason for the project:
> As I have different mailboxes for the business and privatly, I wanted to
> be able to  controll my mail from "anywhere". There are several webmail
> interfaces out, to just  name a few; Horde, Ilohamail, and more and
> there is as well the www.mail2web.com  which I used a lot over the time.
> But I wanted my own one, especially as I am running  my own mailserver
> for over 3 years now, for privat and business. And a small  challenge.
> Everyone was talking Apache, IIS with ISAPI and by searching the
> sqirrelmail sites I  found that someone got it running on xitami CGI.
> And that got me thinking and I just  asked here on the list if someone
> had experience with setting up sqirrelmail on xitami.
>
> I got 2 responses, Ron and Fokke Nauta. Fokke tried it and didn't got it
