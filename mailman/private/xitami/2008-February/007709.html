Hi,

This is to announce the launch of a new wiki project for Xitami.

The site is currently at http://xitami.wikidot.com.

The goal is to migrate all existing documentation for Xitami to this
site and create a new place for the community of Xitami users.  As
many of you have told me, the old site is broken, links like
http://www.imatix.com/html/xitami/index.htm don't work any more.

When the site begins to take shape we'll move it to www.xitami.org and
archive the old site.

Right now, I'm looking for volunteers to help edit the site.  It's
really simple - it's a wiki - and all hands are welcome.

To join the wiki site, register on Wikidot and then join on
http://xitami.wikidot.com/system:join.
