  With help from Johannes...

  In the defaults.cfg or xitami.cfg add the following settings after installing PHP4 and restart your Xitami when done
  ==================================================================================
  Hi,

  here the settings for php.

  [Filter]
      .php = "D:/PHP/php.exe"

  [Mime]
      Php = "application/x-httpd-php"

  In the filter section note the forward slahes. I had to use those, backslash didn't work for me.
