So what you are say is that you don't have anything below cgi-bin/ ie
the root? You could make the site = to cgi-bin and just not have a
cgi-bin. 

Or in your site.cfg you can try this if you want to cgi executed at the
root

   Webpages = "webpages/yoursite.com/"
   Cgi-bin = "webpages/yoursite.com"

Or this,
   Webpages = "webpages/yoursite.com/cgi-bin/"
   Cgi-bin = "webpages/yoursite.com/cgi-bin"


And try 

Default2 = "upload.cgi"

I don't know if it will work or not but it's probably worth a try.
