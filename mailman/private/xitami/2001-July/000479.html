In message <00aa01c11484$386eab20$0201a8c0@midco.net>, "Richard Donahue" writes:
>PHP 4.0.6, Xitami 2.4c3 Pro and Windows NT 4 workstation.
>In filters I have:
>.php=c:/php
>
>In the php.ini I edited:
>doc_root = c:\xitami\webpages
>extension_dir = c:\php
>
>When trying to access a test php all I get is "The page cannot be displayed"

Check cgierr.log.

I strongly suspect you don't actually have a c:\php _program_ for Xitami
to run (which is what you're telling it to run) and that's the problem.

You need to point the filter at the _program_ doing the filtering, not
just a directory that might happen to contain it somewhere.
