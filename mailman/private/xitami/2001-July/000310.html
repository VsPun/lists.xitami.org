David,

You don't buy ip addresses. You purchase internet access from an ISP and they supply
your ip addresses. They buy big blocks and subdivide them. Sort of like a land
developer. You buy a domain name from one of the many places that are now selling them.
You can then have as many subdomains as you want, or that you can afford, depending on
your isp's requirements.
