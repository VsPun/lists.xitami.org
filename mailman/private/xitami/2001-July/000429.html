Whilst such an attitude might be condoned in an end-user, those designing
any web site ought to be conducting testing with at least Netscape (in
addition to IE).  If they're thorough, they ought to try some other
browsers as well, such as Opera, with a text-only browser (such as Lynx)
also used, especially for checking accessibility to the disabled (such as
those using screen-reader software).

"Designing" for IE alone is preventing half the world from having the
hope to see your site.

So even if you personally don't use Netscape whilst "surfing" yourself,
you should at least see how it (mis)behaves with your own site.
