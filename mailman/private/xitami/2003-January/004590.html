it looks as if you are trying to execute a cgi file from you webpages 
directory.  I am not positive, but I believe you can't do that.  Your 
cgi-bin directory should be at the same directory level as your "webpages" 
directory.  Thats where the CGI module looks when its trying to execute your 
scripts.  PHP can have scripts located in any directory path.
