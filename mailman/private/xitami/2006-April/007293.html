I did find the problem.  My shebang line (first line) in the perlssi 
(file in xitami) needed to point to my perl.exe file.  All SSI seems to 
be working now.

I have had good luck splitting tasks between Xitami and Apache under 
win2k3 (millions of small static hits will produce a much smaller load 
on Xitami) as well as better Perl performance (for some reason) runs 
MUCH better on Xitami where PHP runs better under Apache2.  However I 
cannot get good stability on CGI past my Xitami 2.4 .  I'd like to use 
the features in 2.5 but I'll need to wait for some CGI fixes first.
