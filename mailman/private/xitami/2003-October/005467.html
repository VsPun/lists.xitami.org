A forum can give you the best of both worlds. MUCH better organization,
archiving, and searching. AND, most forum scripts let you "subscribe", so
everything still comes in via email. You never have to visit the forum,
unless you want to make a post. And you can post without giving an email
address.

I am a member of a couple of forums and I find myself just reading the
messages.  If I have to login to answer any questions then I usually just
don't.  So if I have to got a webpage, then login, just to answer a question
then the list will suffer.

Just my $.02
