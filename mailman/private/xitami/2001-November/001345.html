Greetings;

with the alias you have below the url would be
http://127.0.0.1/images_esp/something.gif and so on.

No matter where the actual path points to you only use the
alias in the url. And you can't use .. and such to get out
of the tree under the alias. You need to experiment with
simple scripts and simple pages so you can quickly see what
works and what doesn't.

Good Luck!
