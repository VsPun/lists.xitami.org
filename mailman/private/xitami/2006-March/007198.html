I recommend avoiding GSL. It's OK for occasional use but is buggy and
results in crashes for no good reason now and then. I spent a long
tiem writing stuff in GSL that I subsequently rewrote in standrad
perl/CGI becuase the GSL couldn't cope with large XML files and
multiple hits on the same GSL file (at least I think that was the
other bug - I forget it's 3-4 years ago now)

(you can disable the XML processing via CSL by commenting out the line
  .xml=xixxml
in the [WSX] section of xitami.cfg)
