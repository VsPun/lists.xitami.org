After years of running Xitami on a Win98 machine, I moved it over to an XP machine.

It was running fine except that once a day, it crashed on it's own. I check all logs and found
absolutely nothing to indicate why it was doing that.

Well, I decided to run the "Program Compatibility Wizard" and made it Windows 98 compatible.  Guess
what?  It doesn't crash anymore!

Just thought I'd pass that along.
