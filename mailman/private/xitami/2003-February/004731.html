Sounds like you need the Service version of Xitami.  Service are just 
programs that load even if you haven't logged in yet.  Just download and 
install the service while logged in as an adminstrator. It should all be 
thesame are your other versions, just that you don't have Xitami icon in 
the corner anymore. Heck you can even use the same config files from 
your past install.
