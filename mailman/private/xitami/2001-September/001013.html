You probably want to read:

http://lists.xitami.org./pipermail/xitami/2001-September/000967.html

In short: due to the way that Xitami is resolving the URL with the
illegal '%' encoding, it manages to think it should serve up the home
page for the site, so it sends that as a 200 response.  Other than a
little extra traffic, nothing bad happens.
