Trent,

Sorry it seems I did not understand what you were
saying. I thought you meant that a changed PHP file
was in fact changed output, now I understand.

What you probably want to do, is add the following to
the top of your PHP file:

// past date
header("Expires: Mon, 26 Jul 1997 05:00:00 GMT");
// always changed
header("Last-Modified: " . gmdate("D, d M Y H:i:s") .
" GMT");  
// HTTP/1.1
header("Cache-Control: no-store, no-cache,
must-revalidate");  
header("Cache-Control: post-check=0, pre-check=0",
false);
// HTTP/1.0
header("Pragma: no-cache");
