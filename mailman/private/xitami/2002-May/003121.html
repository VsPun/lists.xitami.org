I believe you can solve these issues with proper DNS configs.  I have three
domains sharing the same cfg file:

Xitami Defaults.cfg file:
www.domain.com=domain.cfg
www.domain.net=domain.cfg
www.domain1.com=domain.cfg

But from with in the Alias section for the domain.cfg file you can set
preferences pertaining that address each domain indepently.  But it gets
kind of tricky.

In the DNS I simply have three forward records and of course, only one
reverse record (which is the authority record).  The source/reverse record
is the address that will respond to the clients browser and then the clients
browser will begin talking to the new forward address that matches the
reverse address.

Forward:
www.domain.com    A    192.168.10.10
www.domain.net      A    192.168.10.10
www.domain1.com  A    192.168.10.10

Reverse:
192.168.10.10    SOA    www
