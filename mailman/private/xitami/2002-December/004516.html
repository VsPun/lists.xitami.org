Hi there, I have a webmail running under correo.zonacharrua.com  (or
webmail.domain.com)

First make sure that your "webpages\IlohaMail\source", is define as cgi-bin
directory or path, that (with the proper configuration), will allow
execution of scripts.

Second you must have PHP support installed and configured in Xitami. The
latest instalations of PHP will do just fine.

If you put your Index.php as one of the defaults pages and It continues to
be treated as text, that means your cgi-bin dir + php is not properly
configured.

If you want I can send you a mail, so you can look at my files syntaxis.

Hope you manage..
