I tried it with Xitami and it worked pretty much right from the zip. You 
have to make sure that you are pointing to the correct location. The AXS 
default calls for 

document.write("<IMG SRC="\axs\ax.pl?trans.gif&ref=");

This ASSUMES that you have AXS installed in http://whatever.com/axs

Anywhere else, and you will have to change the directory/location.

I also did notice that it didn't matter if I used \ or /...
