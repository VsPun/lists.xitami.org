Hi,

Don't know if this helps, but i had same problem installing php with Xitami
under Windows... and it was -yes- an unbeliavable incredible minor problem:

I had this in the filter configuration:

[Filter]
    .php=F:\PROGRAMAS\PHP\php.exe

and had to change it to:

[Filter]
    .php=F:\PROGRA~1\PHP\php.exe

Don't ask me why... but it worked after this minor change.
