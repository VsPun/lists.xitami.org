Have you pondered the legality of modifying someone else's computer 
without their consent?   Wouldn't you be suspect for damages, real or 
imagined?  

Some people are going to be really po'd when they discover they have to 
reformat everything on their local network.  As very few good deeds go 
unpunished, I don't think I want them looking in my direction.

reformatting their computer remotely?  Surely you realize that hackers 
are applying their own automated routines but for other purposes?

relay all these gazillions of daily hits to them so they will learn to be 
more responsible than to default bug-ridden internet software in the next
