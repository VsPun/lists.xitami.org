Also, you can do this:

<script language=3D"JavaScript" type=3D"text/javascript">
<!-- Hide script from older browsers

// end hiding contents -->
</script>

or, for PDA/Phone browsing:

<SMALLSCREENIGNORE></SMALLSCREENIGNORE>

As for people hacking your code, server-side helps, but if they want it
they will get it.  You'll just have to be creative and consider the hacking
flattery, because your code is so desirable.
