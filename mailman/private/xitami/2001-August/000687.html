I've answered this question many times recently. I think it should be put in
the FAQ.

Try copying PHP.EXE and PHP4TS.DLL to the Xitami directory and adding the
following to defaults.cfg (or whatever):

[filter]
    .php=php.exe
    .php3=php.exe
    .phtml=php.exe

This works here.
