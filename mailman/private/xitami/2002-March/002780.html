I have it on a P500Mhz with 128 Ram and win2000proSP1.
The same machine has also mail server(merak), firewall(winroute pro),
webmail(merak), ftp(xitami), WAP(merak)
I have less than 300 hits a day on http.
Less than 10/day WAP.
About 60 / week FTP.
28 are sharing the internet connection thru this computer.
About 50 e-mail clients connect every 10-30 minutes(pop/smtp).
(8.00am-7.00pm). Less during the night.
And everithing goes fine, not one single crash since 21/NOV/2001 and it's on
24h/day.
And i do my ouw cgi in delphi and they work fine.
Xitami is one great product. But it should have WAP support.
