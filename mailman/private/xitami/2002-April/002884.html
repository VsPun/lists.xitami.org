stop the xitami service running on port 80.
open the xitami WBA for the new service and change the "ip port base" from
"0" to "5000"
your new xitami service will then start on port 5080 (you may have to
stop/restart).
start the original service back up (it will still be on port 80)

i'm sure there's other ways to do this....
