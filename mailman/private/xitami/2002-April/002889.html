What your trying to do id use FrontPage extensions.  Xitami doesn't support
that protocal.  FP extensions is a special way to change files on a web
server.  I would recomend that you publish your pages with an FTP program.

Xitami does not now, nor will in the future support Front Page extensions. 
This has been a hot topic in the past.  If you're a savy enough programmer
Xitami is open source.  And the FrontPage is actually a documented protocal
so you could in theory build FP compatibility into Xitami.
