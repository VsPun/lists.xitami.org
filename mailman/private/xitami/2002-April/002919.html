Sure...just finished 2 months of intense testing of most of the popular FTP
servers.  Here are my results:

Fastest (by far) although a bit hard to configure:
http://www.raidenftpd.com/  $13.00US standard version
Easiest to get running and very stable:  http://www.texis.com/ $30.00US
Best free server with plenty of options and easy to use:
http://www.nitrolic.com/  (free)
Longest history, stable, good options, reasonably priced:
http://www.serv-u.com  $39.95US
