we have something like that. we just send request to specific ports and see
if a response come back (try telneting to port 25 of a mail server or port
80 of a web server). we do this for business purposes..... but i've added my
own to test my web / mail / camera servers at home  ;-)

as for restarting the apps - that's another application. our stuff is
complex enough that it needs to be researched and fixed by a person. but a
web or mail server should be pretty simple to restart. i have a batch job
running every night for backups. i also restarts mail servers, dns, etc.

any errors are sent to email or pagers.

simple testing isn't that difficult to develop. ewen's post is another good
example of a way to do this.
