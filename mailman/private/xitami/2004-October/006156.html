In the headers of EVERY mail sent from Xitami; see above :-)

In particular, you want <http://lists.xitami.org/mailman/listinfo/xitami>;
if you go there, one of the options is almost at the very end of the
page, and invites one:

    "To change your subscription (set options like digest and delivery
    modes, get a reminder of your password, or unsubscribe from Xitami),
    enter your subscription email address:"
    
One enters the currently subscribed mailbox address and clicks on the
"Options" button on the form; then the next page permits you to select
between setting "Digest Mode" to On or Off.  You must have the former; I
have the latter.  This means that each individual message is delivered to
me separately, rather than waiting for the accumulation throughout 24h.
