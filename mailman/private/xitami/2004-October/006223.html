Just to throw in two bits more for you to contemplate. I had an SE box for
an intranet app in an office once. Had some crash problems occasionally,
only needed a reboot to fix the problem, did not happen very often. The
cause seemed to be SE's inablity to release memory, so may need to research
ways to optimize the ram for the op system. And don't let them tell you that
SE is a bad os, just and older one, one that had a very good rep during it's
time.
