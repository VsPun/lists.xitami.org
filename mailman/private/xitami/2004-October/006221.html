Look in the log folder C:\Xitami\Logs  You will find ac------.log & an
er------.log.  ------ being the date. These files can be opened with notepad &
will tell you what is causing the problem. 

The log file should show if there were any dos requests.
The error file should tell you what happened at that time.

IMO, the win98se crash could have been caused by to many users at one time for
your system to handle. I believe the default number of users for that version of
Xitami is 10 & that may be to many for your system. Try resetting defaults.cfg 
[Server]
    Limit = "10"  to 5 & see if you still have problems
