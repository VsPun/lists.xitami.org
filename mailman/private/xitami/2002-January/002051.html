>He suggests (in dutch): That's a strange content-type, shouldn't you
>set the content-type for .css to "text/css"
>
>I would like to, so how do I change content-type for .css in Xitami?

Add:

[Mime]
   css=text/css

to defaults.cfg and restart Xitami.
