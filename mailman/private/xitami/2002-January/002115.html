There was no switch I could find in Eudora 3 light so I went into the
eudora.ini and added a long line in the [Settings] section.  Now everything
displays cleanly.  Thanks all for pointing me in right direction!

The line so far reads:
TabooHeaders=List-Help,List-Post,List-Subscribe,List-Id,List-Unsubscribe,Lis
t-Archive,Received,Message-ID,X-Authentication-Warning,X-Accept-Language,MIM
E-Version,References,Content-Type,Content-Transfer-Encoding,Errors-To,X-Been
There,X-Mailman-Version,X-MSMail-Priority,X-MIMEOLE,X-MIMETrack,X-Listproces
sor-Version,X-OriginalArrivalTime,X-Priority,X-Sender,Precedence,In-Reply-To
,Content-ID,Content-Disposition,Importance,Delivery-date
