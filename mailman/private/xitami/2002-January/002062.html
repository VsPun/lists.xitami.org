I second that counter.

For an example, go to http://www.vanarts.com which
is on a Xitami server.  The counter at the bottom of the Home Page points
to my Xitami cgi-bin count.exe which is version 2.5 from
http://muquit.com/muquit/software/Count/Count.html

Note that you can use
custom digits, unlike link-to counters.  I have made digits in PaintShop
from GIF images and even from photo JPG images.
