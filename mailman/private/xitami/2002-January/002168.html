I just wrote to another post on this email list that I reboot my *Win98*
server every 80 days as a preventative measure only. It runs Xitami, WarFTP,
a remote access server (like PC Anywhere), a SMTP/POP server, Norton AV
weekly, PHP, plus it serves out a laser printer to the LAN and provides
shared Windows hard drive space.

True, it only services 5 people plus my LAN and elsewhere, about 300 emails
a day, and less than 50 unique web visitors daily, but it has been much
easier to manage and more secure than Mandrake 7 & 8, RedHat 6, NT4, or
2KPro. I'm sure it wouldn't scale that well, but for this, it works great.
