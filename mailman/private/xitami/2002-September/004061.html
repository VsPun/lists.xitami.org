I got this to work about 6 months ago... folks on this list suggested I do
the folloing to get it to work, and it does:

include this in your WML page... you're WML editor will probably do this by
default.
"Content-Type: text/vnd.wap.wml"

AND set your MIME to:

     Wbmp=image/vnd.wap.wbmp
     Wml=text/vnd.wap.wml
     Wmlc=application/vnd.wap.wmlc
     Wmls=text/vnd.wap.wmlscript
     Wmlsc=application/vnd.wap.wmlscriptc
