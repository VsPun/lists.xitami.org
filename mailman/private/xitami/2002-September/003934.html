I'm assuming you mean Xitami for Windows. I have had good luck with 
"Internet Anywhere" mail server. I use an older version, but it has 
served me with well with Xitami on a pitiful P133/Win98 "server" for 
about 2 or 3 years now.


It is a standard pop3/SMTP server that has relay control, mailing list 
support, and even a web-based remote configuration tool, if that's what 
you want.
