At 23:23 16/01/04, you wrote:
>Can I host more than one website (different websites) with one IP address?
>

In your defaults.cfg file (example)

[Virtual-hosts]
     Mail.verfaillie.com=mail.cfg
     Shop.verfaillie.be=shop.cfg
     Shop.verfaillie.com=shop.cfg
     Web.verfaillie.be=verfa.cfg


That's your mail.cfg (complete, example)

[Server]
     webpages=webpages/mail

[Accesslog]
     Filename=mail.log


When somebody go to mail.verfaillie.com, the mail.cfg is checked, points to
the "mail" directory.
Verify that you have correct DNS pointing to your server for all domains.
