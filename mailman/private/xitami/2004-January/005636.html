I had a similar problem and the feedback I remember from Xitami was that the
FTP service was not intended for robust service.  It was suggested that
another FTP server be used.

What I ended up with was War FTP.  It runs as an application or as a
service.  Never have had a hiccup in two years.  You can get it at
http://www.warftp.org/.

If you want to review others, a good source is
http://www.serverwatch.com/stypes/index.php/RlRQ.
