Some things to consider:

clicking on Configuration, clicking on SSI, then making sure the SSI =
enabled is checked on.

I also noticed that (and it might be a typo) that below you have =
Fleece.txt as the filename but when you call it in the SSI it is =
fleece.txt so your case switching may be a problem.
