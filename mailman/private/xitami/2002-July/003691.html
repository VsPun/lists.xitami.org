it is an arcane science, akin to alchemy, getting listed in the engines.
There are folks who will charge you money to do it but here there are 2 esy
ways, aside from the meta-tag and wait method.

1) make firends with the appropriate category editor at dmoz.org, the Open
Directory Project.  Once a site is in there (well, after a couple weeks) it
get's populated to all the sites that license the directory.  Categories are
edited by volunteers.  If there isn't someone for your category, volunteer
yourself.

2) sign up for Lycos's InSite service which get's you into their version of
the FAST catalog, and will also give you a search utility to run on your
site.  There are other services, but an independant survey found lycos to be
the best for the money.

3) go to searchenginewatch.com and read up on it... many many strategies
there.

good luck
