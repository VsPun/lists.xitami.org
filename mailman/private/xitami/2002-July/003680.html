At 05:55 PM 7/15/2002 -0330, jon writeth:
>Do you happen to have any Perl syntax for sending and e-mail through a
script for your 
>program smtpsend?

Use the system() command.  Be sure to grab the output (if any) and display
it to the user.  If there is no output, the e-mail was sent successfully.
Nuclear Vision allows for refined control of the e-mail building and
sending process with detailed error messages when they occur.

Type smtpsend -? at the command-line for detailed help.  Also, the
readme.txt file describes program usage.  SMTPSEND is a sub-set freeware
program of Nuclear Vision and only demonstrates a very small portion of the
power of Nuclear Vision.
