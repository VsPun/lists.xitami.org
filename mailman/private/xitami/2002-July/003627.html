If I'm not mistaken what your asking is 'Where do I put the files'.  There is a folder at 'C:\XITAMI\WEBPAGES'.  You can get rid of everything inside the folder and put your stuff there.  Thats it for files.

As to logging in from a library and seeing yourself, you'll have to know your public side IP#.  To you it may look like 127.0.0.1, to the outside world you look like '213.52.0.1'.  I put up a little tool that you use to find your public side IP# at http://yumyum.ws/ipplayground.php  Just go there on your server machine and write down your number.  Remeber each time you log off and back on the internet this public side IP# will change.  You can't trust what a program called 'WINIPCFG.EXE' says is your IP#/  Your ISP maybe doing some funky stuff to keep you from running a server.

Other little tidbits about webservers in general:
	127.0.0.1 and the words localhost are the same thing to a computer.
	If want to play around with your setting type in 'http://127.0.0.1/admin'
	Xitami does not do most server side scripting when you install it.  Your have to install things like PHP and Cold Fushion
	Windows based webservers don't care about case(uppercase and lowercase) on file names.  Example: http://127.0.0.1/MYFile.htm is the same as http://127.0.0.1/myfile.htm to a Windows based webserver.  Unix and Linux servers are not so forgiving.  Remember that if your going to put these files onto another server watch your case.
/
