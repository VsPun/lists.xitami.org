PLEASE do not post in HTML to this mailing list; indeed, you should never
post in HTML to *any* mailing list (or Usenet newsgroup).  

Incidentally, you are mailing in *only* HTML, without the alternative
plain-text portion.  Whilst I don't advocate dual-mailing (i.e. in plain
ASCII *and* in HTML) either, since it needlessly increases a message's
size by, on average, three- or four-fold, you should note that many
anti-spam protection measures automatically deem "pure-HTML" as being
pure-SPAM.
