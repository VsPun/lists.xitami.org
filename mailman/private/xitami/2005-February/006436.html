Well,

I do think the Xitami Email list should be changed to a better forum with
searches, posting, replies, threads, etc.  Xitami email list is extremely
outdated.  On another note, I do use IRC all the time - from home and from
work - because it is a great instant resource.  With IRC you don't need to
wait hours for answers.   Having been a user for quite some time, though, I
do recommend using another base server besides irc.freenode.net.  I was on
it last night and realized that the freenode.net server is very isolated
from the rest of the IRC servers.  On the other servers no one was on
#xitami - I had to switch over to freenode.net in order to find Mr. Thomas
who recommended we do this to begin with.

I use irc.qeast.net and irc.mindspring.com = they are very well connected
and tap you into the largest IRC communities.  There are plenty other
popular ones as well but those are the most convenient for me.  Someone
should create a bot for the #xitami channel too, that way there is always a
presence on the channel.

I also believe if the iMatix folks simply list it next to their support
information that Xitami users will automatically gravitate to it.  An iMatix
endorsement is what its going to take for any forum to work.  Its like
asking "who wants to play basketball?" versus "who wants to play basketball
with Michael Jordan?" - which do you think will get a bigger turnout?
