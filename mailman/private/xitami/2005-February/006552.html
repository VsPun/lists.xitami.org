You don't need to do that. As I desribed in my previous post, you enable the "virtual ftp 
server" in the advanced tab in the router under
1. open the router GUI in the browser
2. click advanced tab
3.scroll down, mark the preconfigured virtual ftp server
4.it should be now shown in the fields above with name ect. You ONLY have to put in 
the ip of your ftp server machine, something like 192.168......NOT the public ip !!
5. just click enable
6. click "apply"
7. in the next window "save settings ?"
8.click " yes"
9. Now your ftp server is ready to get connections.
