Just a note here, don't confuse attacks with Search Engine Web Crawlers. The
ones that Alex showed clearly appear to be searching for executables. Not
something a Search Engine would be looking for. My problem was when the MSN
search bot hit my sites indexing every page, every image and every day in
the calendar. And although you generally want the search engines to find
your site, I expect not at the expense of eating up bandwidth and slowing
down your server.
