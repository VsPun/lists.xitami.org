> how do i get off this list?

You can check out any time you like, but you can never leave!

As the numerous footers explain, go to
http://lists.xitami.org/mailman/listinfo/xitami

Scroll to the bottom, it will say, "To change your subscription (set
options like digest and delivery modes, get a reminder of your password,
or unsubscribe from Xitami), enter your subscription email address:" so
you would enter your address and click the Edit Options button. Then on
the Xitami Configuration page that should come up, enter your password
and click Unsubscribe.
