As far as I know there are no major security risks with Xitami, I have used
it for several years without any problem. There are a few Denial of service
vulnerabilities in older versions of xitami but I think most are patched
now. I love Xitami so much I purchased the pro version. There are several
advantages to running Xitami; speed, stability, ease of configuration, but I
think one major advantage of running Xitami is often overlooked. That
advantage is that there is such a small installed base out there that there
is very little incentive for hackers target it.
