In message <000001c186fe$81238b50$6400a8c0@COWBOY>, "Frank Harrell" writes:
>Howdy, Does anyone know if it is possible to have Xitami listen on 2
>ports (80 & 443) at the same time? 

No, Xitami can't do this (listen on two ports at once).  (It'd be handy, 
but it can't at present.)

BTW, Xitami/Pro will list on port 80 (http) and port 443 (https) at
once, since it supports SSL.  Xitami itself doesn't support SSL though;
it's a feature only in the Pro version.

The typically solution to listening on two ports at once is to run two
copies of Xitami.  Providing they're in different directories, and have
all the other services disabled/moved to different ports (FTP, LRWP,
etc) they should work together.
