sad news for both the Xitami web server and ProtoNova (both servers get hit
equally with this one).  Today has been a strange day indeed.  Those who
are developing servlets for LRWP-capable servers (e.g. Xitami) using the
Sokrates servlet engine will probably need to find or develop an
alternative or switch to the Sokrates' group's "ZeroToaster" environment:
