The suggestions I gave in my last post was specific to my setup.  And,
yes, www is required in order to use port forwarding in dns2go.  I know
it is not required in general, but it is what the dns2go sever program
specifically looks for in order to know what to port forward and not
port forward.  In other words dns2go will not port forward
http://mydomain.com  , but it will port forward http://www.mydomain.com
.  It all ends up at the same ip, but only the one with the www prefix
gets port forwarded by dns2go (in other words its specific to dns2go).
