To the list operators: maybe it will be a better alternative to use some 
sort of bulletin board (ultiboard is a good one) for postings. (example: 
http://www.powerbasic.com/support/forums/Ultimate.cgi). I once created such 
a bulletin board and it's easy to maintain.

Your mailbox is not flooded with mails you probably won't read, the threads 
are separated by subject.
When i first used a bulletin board, i didn't like the idea (impossible to 
attach documents, difficult to quote and edit text, ...) but after a while 
i began to appreciate the board.
You can get a notification whenever somebody respond to your posting and 
you can check the board whenever you want, not when you check your mail.
And you can create different forums for those who only work with 
windows/unix/mac/...
