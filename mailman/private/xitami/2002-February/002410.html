At 12:06 PM 2/14/2002 +1300, daniel carter writeth:
>So apparently running xitami in turbo mode will damage your hardware.

No, that's not what the article says.  The only way your hardware will be
damaged is if it runs in an uncirculated environment and the CPU overheats
or has electrical problems.  In either of those cases you have a more
fundamental problem than just Xitami.

There is nothing wrong with running Xitami in turbo mode.  However, as a
result of running it in turbo mode, it *will* skew benchmarking statistics
as it gets higher thread priority than both IIS and Apache.
