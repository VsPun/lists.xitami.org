> I want to disable the webserver and just use the ftp daemon

While I like Xitami's FTP module, it is hardly a full-featured
FTP server. And as Thomas said, it isn't meant to be run apart
from the HTTP server. 

There are LOTs of decent stand-alone FTP servers out there. Most
are more complete FTP machines than the Xitami module. 

See http://serverwatch.internet.com/ftpservers.html for a fairly
good listing.
