You missed yesterday's (08/14/2002) security issue dealing with Xitami/Pro
and basically anything using OpenSSL (ProtoNova is mentioned, but Apache is
also vulnerable).

For those who missed the message, there are 4-6 very serious security
issues (all documented somewhere on bugtraq) and anything from 0.9.6f and
lower is vulnerable to some sort of DoS/DDoS/Buffer Overflow attack.
Anyone running Xitami/Pro *MUST* upgrade their OpenSSL libraries to avoid a
hostile takeover and/or DoS.

I forgot to mention that this affects ALL users of Xitami/Pro (not just
Windows users).
