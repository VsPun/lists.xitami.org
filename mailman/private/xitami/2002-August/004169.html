One thing to look for:  If you move your "webpages" and "cgi-bin" folders 
from their default locations, make sure that the paths do not have any 
spaces in the names of the files or folders.  I was having problems such as 
you described after I decided to move my work to a folder with a name like 
"my site"

There's nothing in the Xitami documentation about this.  I would have been 
tearing my hair out if I had any.  A search of this list's archives saved me.
