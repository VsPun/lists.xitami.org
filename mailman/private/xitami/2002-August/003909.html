> The Admin script tells me that "The value for the PATH_TRANSLATED
> environment variable [c:/website] is incorrect.

Open talkadm.cgi in NotePad and look at these lines:

# For environments where $ENV{'SCRIPT_NAME'} is not defined by the webserver,
# uncomment the line below, after modifying it to suit your environment
#
#$ENV{'SCRIPT_NAME'} = "/cgi-bin/talkadm.cgi";
#
# For environments where $ENV{'PATH_TRANSLATED'} is not defined by the webserver,
# uncomment the line below, after modifying it to suit your environment
#
#$ENV{'PATH_TRANSLATED'} = "../cgi-bin/talkadm.cgi";

A sample Xitami output I have here shows PATH_TRANSLATED as "C:/mw",
without trailing slash or script name. The above seems to want a
full file specification. I hate to hard-code file specifications,
but try editing as the comments say.
