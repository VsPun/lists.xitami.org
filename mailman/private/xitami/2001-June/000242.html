What's the equivalent of the shebang ("#!") syntax for Win32?
Unfortunately, Win32 platforms don't provide the shebang syntax, or anything
like it.

Although Win32 systems themselves don't use the shebang, the Apache
webserver does. If you are using the Apache webserver on your Win32 system
you should put the shebang line at the top of your CGI scripts.

Your shebang line should look something like:

 #!perl
