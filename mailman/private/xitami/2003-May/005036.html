I know it sounds strange but older PCI video cards could also corrupt  
files on a LAN.  We spent 3 months determining that our LAN problems 
were caused by video drivers.  If you happen to be using an old PC 
because it runs Xitami just fine it could be along shot answer.  We 
serve on a T1 and haven't seen a problem with either FTP or HTTP, but we 
have very little traffic.
