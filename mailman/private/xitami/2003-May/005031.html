740 k bits /second.
It seems to corrupt large files of any extension.

In the past, I have not had problems with Xitami FTP and have used it successfully.
When the speed of the connection went from 256 kbits /sec. to 740 kbits/ sec.
is when I believe (think) the problem started occuring.
Right when we got the software done and started winding down testing !!!!!!

Anyway ... now I am burning a CD ROM and copying from the CD to the web site.

Since I wasn't using FTP any more, I turned it off in the .CFG and restarted Xitami.
Downloaded the software again, and the Setup.Exe works.

I'll keep my eyes on this, but I believe the problem is now solved. It appears that FTP
just has to be turned on ... not used ... to get the high speed problem.

No doubt you all have discussed this 100 times and I foolishly didn't hear.
