> That's not enough, you will need to have a dns entry pointing to your
> laptop. Contact your isp if he's doing your dns. Easy way is to change
> the local ip address to the one of the former server.

An easier way is to edit the HOSTS file. Add an entry that reads 
127.0.0.1            www.thunting.com
(or local.thunting.com or whatever) and it will work. The HOSTS file 
is located in 
[windows directory]\system32\drivers\etc
