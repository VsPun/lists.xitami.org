Paul Reid wrote:
> 
> Open a browser on the same machine, open http://localhost ,
> 
> *some* versions of Windows are reluctant even to open "localhost"
> until they check with a DNS server. 

Add a file \Windows\hosts with:

127.0.0.1	localhost 

or just open http://127.0.0.1

You could even put other names into the hosts file:

127.0.0.1	myownsite.me

and then open http://myownsite.me
