the internal version and the downloadpage synchron. RC8 is the

- experimental support for ServletContext#getResource(...)

- some methods of HttpServletRequest returning a platform
specific path now (sometimes we got a mixture of / and \)

- instead of defining a config-file
(config=c:\\somewhere\\config.ini) we can add also
"config.variable=value" in servlet.ini (the servlet will see it
as "variable=value" after calling .init(..)
