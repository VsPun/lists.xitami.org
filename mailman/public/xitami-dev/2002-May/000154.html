On Tue, 7 May 2002 23:43:41 +0800, Phillip Wee wrote:

hi phillip,

> It appears that the settings in servlet.properties were not 
> read, and defaults were assumed by the gnujsp servlet.

yes, there is the problem. our servletrunner doesn't use the
"blah.blah.parameter" (which is a nice idea but practically
useless).

your servlet.ini should contain "config = servlet.properties"
(without quotes) and servlet.properties should contain

scratchdir=c:\\somewhere\\mytempdir\\
debug=true

to get an idea what is parsed and what not.

if it doesn't help i will setup GNUJSP for further testing.
