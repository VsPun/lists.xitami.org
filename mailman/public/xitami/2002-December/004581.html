LRWP apps are not like most other CGI types--they are simply TCP/IP programs
connecting to a certain Xitami port. And as such, developping LRWPs should be
pretty much platform-independent. If you can use the socket components in
Delphi, then I think you can develop LRWPs. Read through the LRWP section of the
Xitami manual, if you haven't already. That will give you the best answer IMO.
