In message <003b01c12942$91fd6e10$0d00a8c0@dt1.sdca.home.com>, "Jeff Hamblin" writes:
>[webmask in Xitami accepts, and sends forbidden]
>What I really want to do is deny any connection at all from certain IPs,
>just ignore them (sending no error message).
>Is this possible with 2.4d9?

No. What you want is a firewall; then you can configure it to drop
traffic from sites you don't like (and thus completely ignore them).

Because of the way that Xitami works it has to accept the connection to
find out who is at the other end, and at that point it'd be very rude to
just hang up on them, so it sends a HTTP-style response back.
