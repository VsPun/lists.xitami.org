Hi Brad,

This is an old one that I fell into as well. Simply move php.exe and php4ts.dll
into the Xitami directory (or possibly php4ts.dll to winnt\system32\ or
equivalent depending on your windows flavour) and remove the paths from the
beginning of the Xitami filter line entry.

Re-start Xiami and all will being to function as expected, one day when I have
time I'll sit down & see why this is so :-).

I was given this info from somebody else on this mailing list, it appears fairly
frequently.

Best of Luck,
