This is an ordinary DNS look-up. The 207.69.188.186 address is similar
to the one you used to mail your note, so I assume it is your ISP's DNS

want to know who 192.168.0.2 is. 

I'm not sure I understand the answer it gets, and I'm not sure Xitami
liked it. The "packet size error" is also odd.
