I'm not sure what you want, but if you want to hide the real address, you 
can use frames.

That's how i'm doing it

<index.htm> of virtual domain:
<html>
<frameset frameborder="no" rows="0,*">
    <frame scrolling="no">
    <frame src="http://web.verfaillie.com:119/mail">
</frameset>
</html>

(i'm using port 119 on the same domain (and same server) for my webmail 
server)).
Have a look at <http://mail.verfaillie.com>
