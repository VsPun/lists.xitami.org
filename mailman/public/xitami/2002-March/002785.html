My current server is a AMD K6-2 366Mhz (was 333) with 62MB ram and 40GB WD
IDE ATA66 hard drive, before I cannibalized it and rebuilt it, it was a HP
Pavilion 4440. I'm running WinXP Pro with M-Daemon Mail Server, GuildFTPd
and VNC. After two weeks of uptime I didn't notice a bit of slow down with
the system nor did Xitami take up more than 2MB of physical memory (system
floats around 46 - 54).

PHP and MySQL is very fast on the system, pages load almost instantly over
the LAN. Hosts person websites for now. http://dewdude.ath.cx

I had it on a Win98SE machine on Pentium 200 with 32MB RAM (a Compaq
Presario 4504) and it ran decently.
