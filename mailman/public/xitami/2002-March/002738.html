I solved it !! (tried a lot of things)

I removed the "webmask" section from the "default.cfg" file... 

The * sign (exclude all) somehow got on a single line and excluded
everybody including myself.

I had put the * sign at the end of an IP address to exclude a series of
IP's.. but when I restarted the server the line was automatic cut and
the * was placed on the last line alone. Xitami read this as "exclude
all" and effectivly shut the server down....
