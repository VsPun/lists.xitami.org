>Question: If I want to install another Xitami in say PC#2 for this intranet,

>won't fight with PC#1's Xitami

I solve this by going  from ISP > Modem > Hub > Router
I plug Router into Hub. Hub into Modem.
The  external PC is connected to HUB, internal machines
connected to router.

External PC is given a dedicated IP that I have purchased.
Internal PC's get IP from router.
