> I thought it was a good idea to also make folders called "webpages2" and "webpages3" for
my different PHP learning projects.

[alias]
cats=E:\xitami\webpages2
dogs=E:\xitami\webpages3

Aliases are sometimes helpful for simplifying URLs to static pages.

They do NOT work well for anything complicated. In this case, Xitami
knows that cats=webpages2, but PHP does not know that. There are
workarounds for some situations, but that may be too complicated or
just more trouble than is worth for your situation.

For your situation, why not just rename the webpages2 folder to
"cats"?
