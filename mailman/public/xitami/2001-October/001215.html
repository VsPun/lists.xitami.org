Guessing in the dark: Is your machine Windows? (What version?) Is the
host on unix? NT?

A limit like 10K sounds like your script is passing the body of the file
through the command line and "environment". That could be less than 1K
in DOS, somewhat bigger in Win9x, very large in WinNT, and large and
often essentially unlimited in unix. 

I'm not familiar enough with HTTP-PUT or PHP to know how to check or
modify that... I'm just hoping this jogs the memory of someone who is
more expert in such things.
