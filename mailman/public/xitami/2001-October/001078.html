Hi,

I think you'll have to modify the first line of your cgi-scripts.
On Windows-System it will be something like #!c:\perl\bin\perl.exe,
or wherever you instllalled Perl.
On most Unix-Systems Perl will be found at /usr/local/bin/ , so change
that line to #!/usr/local/bin/perl .
