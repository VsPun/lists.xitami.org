On Fri, 23 Nov 2001 20:15:08 -0000, Ben Sadler wrote:

> how did you get sub-domains to work on xitami? next year i want to move my
> domain to my computer, so that nameservers are running on my computer (its
> cheaper) and i would like to know how to do this.

as any other subdomain too: you define the subdomain in your DNS (like os2.harddiskcafe.de, www.harddiskcafe.de, ftp.harddiskafe.de) and create an entry in the [Virtual-Hosts] section of the xitami configuration.

os2.harddiskcafe.de=c:\www\vhosts\os2.harddiskcafe.de\vhost.cfg

in this vhost.cfg you define the differences in the configuration:

[Server]
    ipaddress=62.159.157.34
    webpages=c:\www\vhosts\os2.harddiskcafe.de\htdocs\
[AccessLog]
    filename=c:\www\vhosts\os2.harddiskcafe.de\logdata\access.log
   cycle-arg=c:\www\vhosts\os2.harddiskcafe.de\logdata\ac%yy%mm%dd.log
[ErrorLog]
    filename=c:\www\vhosts\os2.harddiskcafe.de\logdata\access.log
   cycle-arg=c:\www\vhosts\os2.harddiskcafe.de\logdata\ac%yy%mm%dd.log
[Security]
    filename=c:\www\vhosts\os2.harddiskcafe.de\vhost.aut
    dirlist=1
