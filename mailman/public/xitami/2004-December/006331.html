IIS is Internet Information Services
The integrated web server that comes with XP and NT based O/S from Windows.
The IIS verision on XP and W2K is no. 5 and W2K3 is IIS 6.
They work slightly different.

You need to

right click the my computer icon on your desktop
Select MANAGE
Click Services And Applications
Select the Services Icon on the left hand side..

Look for the listing IIS Admin Service
Right click it in the right hand screen.
Select PROPERTIES
Under startup type, select DISABLED instead of AUTOMATIC
Click STOP
Click OK

That should do it.
