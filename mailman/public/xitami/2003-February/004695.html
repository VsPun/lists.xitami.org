I looked at it real quick, here's my diagnosis, I may be wrong.
You page uses a frameset, with Index.html being the base frame. All =
other pages load as a subset of Index.html, so the browser always shows =
Index.html as the location, because, well, it is! :-)
I didn't see anything on your page which actually used the capability =
for frames, so you might want to get rid of them and save yourself some =
headaches.
