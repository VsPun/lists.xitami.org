Russel Olinger wrote, On 2007-08-13 00:43:

> from the US and I need a way of using GeoWebalizer to show me city 
> origination - anyway to get that to work???  I have read all 
> documentation and haven't found a way to do it.

I have no idea with that specific function, but generally, it's up to 
the ISP to report/register an IP range -- some allow "tracing" down to 
city level, some have only a full "country" range. For more info, muck 
around at www.maxmind.com

If an IP is registered to a smaller corporation, you might be able to 
locate the actual street address it sits at -- www.ripe.net will give 
you that info. That is the case for my own web/mail server, for example.
