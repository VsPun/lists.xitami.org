> After the uninstall of the service version and a reinstall of the
> program version, I get an error

Did you re-boot? Services stay active until you kill them.

Alternatively, you can kill a running Service in Control Panel,
Services (in NT4; it may have moved in Win2000). While there, you
can mark it "Manual Start" so it will never run until you demand it.
That should not be needed if UNinstalled and re-booted, but is a way
to block unwanted Services that won't go away.
