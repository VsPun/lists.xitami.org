Xitami FTP is ok for low usage.  However under high bandwidth conditions (my 100 mbps 
network) I was getting corruption to files.  Low bandwidth corruption only happened rarely 
though some of my users clients for some reason couldn't connect to it.  So for me I went with 
a stand alone ftp client and use Xitami for my web services.
