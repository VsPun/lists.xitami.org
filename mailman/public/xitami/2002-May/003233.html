There's a school of thought regarding running a name service daemon that
if one isn't totally confident of what everything does, one shouldn't
even be contemplating doing it :-)

Can you beg, borrow, steal (or buy) a copy of "DNS and BIND", by Cricket
Liu & Paul Albitz?  It's published by O'Reilly, and is well worth the
money.  DON'T buy the version for WindowsNT (unless you're contemplating
running the NT named); the "ordinary" version provides a much better
insight into just why any (or all:-) of the RRs exist.
