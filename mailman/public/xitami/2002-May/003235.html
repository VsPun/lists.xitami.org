The file provided is, as you've discovered, a *sample* hosts file.  To
make it into a real one, you need to edit it as appropriate for your
situation, and also RENAME it so that its name is *actually* just "hosts"
--- without anything after the "dot".  (I thought that at least one of the
comments in the file actually said that you need to do this: perhaps M$
is making things even more obscure in newer releases?)

Perhaps your system is hiding known extensions, or something; try doing a
DIR in an MS-DOS command prompt, rather than farting about with the GUI.
