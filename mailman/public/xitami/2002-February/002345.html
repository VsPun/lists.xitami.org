Absolutely not, you just have to ensure that

     1. the webuser has execute privileges in the cgi-bin directory
     2. points to the cgi executable
     3. Your cgi executable is reading it's stdin etc correctly

You may have to resort to pop up messages or text log files produced by your cgi
program to let you know if xitami is actually calling your executable.
