>In what order do the files load.  Does Xitami.cfg load as well as
>defaults.cfg?  Or does only the defaults.cfg load.  

xitami.cfg loads defaults.cfg, and defaults.cfg generally loads your
virtual hosts, etc.  I'd definitely suggest looking in the error logs
for Xitami to see if you can find the reason it won't load -- xitami.log
is probably the most useful one to start with.
