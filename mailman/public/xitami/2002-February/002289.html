> The mailing list currently rejects posts bigger than 30KB in size.


	An HTML MIME message comes in an alternate envelope which also includes the TXT
piece. One possible workaround is to extract the first TXT piece and throw away
HTML's. This way HTML e-mail is automatically discarded, and just the txt part
survives.

	Maybe listservers have this option already ?

	The problem is when e-mail starts in HTML, then replies will probably come in
HTML too. And in these cases it can be hard to clip pieces, depending on your
e-mail program.

	Plain TXT e-mail should be fine for this list.
