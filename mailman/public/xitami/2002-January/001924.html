>  how do I get xitami to serve these DjVu file types?

> Xitami wants to download rather than serve the files.

I think it would be correct to say your _browser_ wants to
download the file. Either way, Xitami is offering to serve the
file... your browser either knows what to do (and most browsers
don't know what Djvu is) or save it for you to deal with later.

Have you installed a Djvu reader in your browser? Do you expect
all your surfers to already have Djvu support? 

The standard technique seems to be to use EMBED to force download
of a reader that knows what Djvu is; see: 

http://www.djvuzone.org/support/tutorial/chapter-authoring2.html
