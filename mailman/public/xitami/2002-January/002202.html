In your linksys, you should be able to drop the DMZ if your not gaming or
webcam and just go to the IP forwarding menu in lynksis admin and set the
ports 80 and 21 open to the machine IP address your hosting with.  This way
your IP address will be less available for exploit on the remaining ports.
