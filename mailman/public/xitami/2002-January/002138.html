On Wed, Jan 23, 2002 at 10:36:16PM -0800, Carl Bartlett wrote:
> How do i get xitami to detect my ip address such that i will open a
> connection on the address of the external router?  Im behind the router
> and am getting ip addresses of 10.0.0.3 and 127.0.0.1 How do i set it up
> so that i can connect to xitami for the ip address given to the router?

I'm not sure I understand appropriately but..

I assume your router does Network Address Translation (NAT). So, the
address at the external end of the router will be forwarded (and
translated) to your machine behind onto the address given by the router.
When you open a connection to the address of your router it will end in
your machine whatever IP address it has got.

Furthermore, You can make any program (and Xitami also) to listen on all
of your interfaces online (ethernet, local:127.0.0.1, ...) pointing it to
0.0.0.0

Hope this helps
Laszlo
