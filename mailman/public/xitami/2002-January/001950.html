In message <so0c3uckqb06vb554nf64onf4pc6vt8gpc@4ax.com>, Bruce writes:
>Does Xitami have ALL necessary software built into it to handle CGI scripts?

What do you mean by "ALL"?  It doesn't have half a dozen language
interpreters built in if that is what you are getting at.

Xitami does support most common CGI programs, although it has onlimited
non-parsed-header support, and it doesn't support flushing output to the
client before the end of the CGI run.  There are also reports that some
variable definitions are different from what is expected.
