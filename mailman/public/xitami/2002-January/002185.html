Use dns2go from www.dns2go.com .  Its free, and it optionally does automatic
port forwarding for anything with a www. prefix in front.  So, your site
would be http://www.mysite.com, and your ftp would be ftp://mysite.com .  I
have yet to figure out a way to get ftp://www.mysite.com to work, but I
think that is because it forwards anything with www. in it to the port you
specify, so the ftp request is not sent to the proper port.  I have to admit
that I haven't spent much time on it, so there my still be a way to do it.
