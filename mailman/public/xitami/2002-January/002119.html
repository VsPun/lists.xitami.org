It means that some moron running IIS has allowed his system to be
infected by one of a number of viruses (CodeRed, Nimda, etc) which then
probe the IP address space looking for other idiots also running

like the continuing expansion of your log file.  I would suggest
identifying the ISP(s) of the connecting machine(s) so that you can tell
them, and they in turn can inform their customer.
