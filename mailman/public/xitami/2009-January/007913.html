Xitami community,

I too am excited to hear about Xitami/5. We have been using it bundled with
our product since the OS/2 days of 1993 or so.  It has served us well except
for two things: 1) the ftp server started crashing with heavy usage and 2)
Recently we have mysterious locks up of the server. We don't care about the
ftp server, since we can throw on something like Filezilla. The lock ups are
a problem and have caused us to consider re-writing our application as an
ISAPI plugin.

The only catch with Xitami/5 is that need LRWP support, which I'm sure
nobody is interested in and therefore would be last on the list, or not on
the list at all. We would be willing to look at something like FAST CGI or
maybe writing a plug in.
