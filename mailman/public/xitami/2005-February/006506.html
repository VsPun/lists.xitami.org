Yes you can do this. Put the two in different directories and modify
the portbase of the second one.
In defaults.cfg do the following where XXX is the portnumber you want minus 80
[server]
portbase=XXX

On the other hand are you SURE you want to do this? it may be better
to use Xitami's virtual hosts feature
