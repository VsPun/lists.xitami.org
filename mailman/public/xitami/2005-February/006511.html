I'm not receiving multiples copies.
It could be a faulty connection between your mail client and the POP-3 
server causing the mail to be downloaded multiples times.
A typical POP-3 session involves the download of the messages on the server 
and as last step the deletion of the messages on the server when all 
mesages are downloaded successfully.

Check your mail client to see if it has a setting "dedupe messages".
Check also that the setting "leave mail on server" is OFF

If it still occurs, unsubscribe and subscribe again to the list. Maybe this 
will help.
