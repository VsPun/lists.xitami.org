IRC channels can easily be logged and viewed on a webpage. Mailinglists 
can easily be placed on a webpage also and a forum can easily be tweaked 
to correspond with the mailinglist. There could be a special forum where 
a weekly or daily digest of the list was posted for everyone to read.

Let people find the information they want where they want and how they 
want. That is the key to a healthy and active community. Restricting 
information to one format is not.
