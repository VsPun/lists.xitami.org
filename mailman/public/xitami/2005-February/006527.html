Q.  Do I setup a 2nd account that is mysite.yi.org:9948 and forward 1st acct 
to
     the new acct?

A. Yes, only it's not a new account, it's a new host.

    Make the 2nd hostname "mrsite2.yi.org" or something. Update your IP on 
that host.

   On the 1st host (mysite.yi.org), make the ip 64.141.0.64 (yi.org's 
forwarding ip), and make the url "http://mysite2.yi.org:9948/". yi.org will 
forward all requests to http://mysite.yi.org/ to that url.
