Hi, i've sucessfully installed an run phpnuke( more exactly postnuke v.064) 
with xitami on win2k.

What you most probably get is WARNINGS _NOT ERRORS. This is php-related and 
occurs only due to sloppy php syntax (usage of single quotes instead of 
double quotes).
Try setting your php.ini settings to something like show_errors = ERRORS 
instead of ALL and most of them will vanish.

There is one problem with redirects in case you don't install your project 
into the (webserver) root directory, which you most likely want to do. To 
fix this I've submitted a fix to the postnuke project site somwhere at 
sourceforge.

Of course there are some minor bugs, it's still in development, but I 
consider it to be a way cool tool and suggest to give it a more thorough try.
