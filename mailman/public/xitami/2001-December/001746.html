By far your weakest link is the dsl(128 up).  We ran over a year with a dual
channel ISDN for the same 128 up on a Pentium Pro 200mhz and 256mb ram.
Xitami is very processor and memory friendly.

Your low bandwidth is the stumbling block, but as long as you keep the
graphics down to under 50k per page and don't use any audio, video, flash et
cetera, you can serve quite a number of concurrent users.
