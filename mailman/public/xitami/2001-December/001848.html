Response

With Windows, if installing, ununstalling and fiddling ... consider
a clean format of the drive and a fresh install with all known (by you)
to work device drivers.    

The build of the OS ... in particular those
builds that 'burn' the OS image onto the drive ... can make a big difference.

Your weird behaviour is an indication of a need to service pack the OS
or rebuild it fresh.

Always good to ChkDsk the hard drive on a crash and certainly ... delete your
temp files.

Win 95, 98 and ME are not ideal environments for servers. ( In particular when you got
the MP3 jamming)

I'd look at the OS.
