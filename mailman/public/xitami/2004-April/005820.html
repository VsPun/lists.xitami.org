The problem with blacklists is that sometimes legitimate mail servers get
put on the list. For instance I run 12 domains with Xitami on a dynamic dns.
This automatically makes all my domains invalid for sending email to, for
instance, AOL users. I haven't had any other problems lately but have in the
past and just try getting your domain de-listed when you are on a dynamic
IP. It's we, the General Public, that is suffering because of ISP's
unwilling to shut down spammers. What happens when you block emails for your
customers and someone misses an important message. Are you prepared to
suffer the legal ramifications? I'm not, so I will leave it to the customer
to weed thru the spam.
