Hi Folks,

We (iMatix) are in the middle of moving some domains from one DNS server
to another DNS server.  One of the domains being moved is "xitami.org",
as used by lists.xitami.org for this mailing list.

It is possible that for a (hopefully) short period of time during this
move the domain xitami.org, and hence lists.xitami.org, may be
inaccessible, at least to some people.

To help ensure that the mailing list remains available during this
period, I have configured "lists.xitami.com" (note ".com", not ".org")
as a backup name for the mailing list, viz:

http://lists.xitami.com/mailman/listinfo/xitami

xitami@lists.xitami.com

If you have trouble with the main address (lists.xitami.org) this week 
please try the lists.xitami.com address instead.
