I have set up something like you are asking about.

In the defaults.cfg file, under [Virtual-hosts] I added:
other.mydomain.com = "other.cfg"

In the other.cfg file, under [Server] I added:
Cgi-bin = "c:/Other/cgi-bin"
Webpages = "c:/Other"

You will have to add a zone for other.mydomain.com.
