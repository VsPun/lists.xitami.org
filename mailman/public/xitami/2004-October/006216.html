Hello Merilene,

ProtoNova has a really good anti-Denial of Service architecture built
into
the server.  Xitami can't counter these attacks except at the IP
level
(meaning you have to be awake manning the server).  ProtoNova can
automatically detect a DoS and stop it (even while you sleep).  You
can do
Xitami-style IP mask blocking as well, but you could be blocking
legitimate
IPs.  The anti-DoS architecture's default settings aren't perfect,
but ever
since it went in place, no one using ProtoNova has had a problem.
