You are just running parallell web servers. This is nothing new, and 
in fact, you have not managed to get ASP running on Xitami. The only 
benefit of this setup (over an all-IIS approach) is higher 
performance for static documents served by Xitami and that benefit is 
easily outweighed by the numerous disadvantages (coding hassles 
keeping track of different servers -- absolute links are needed, no 
unified search).

Frankly, this discussion doesn't even belong on the Xitami list. If 
you managed to get ASP running as a CGI filter, that's another thing.
