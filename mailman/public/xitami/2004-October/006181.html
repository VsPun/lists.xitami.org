To Scott and Ron and all others.
Here's a little hint to all about ASP and VBS.

The nasty part about VBS is that you must specify the " Script 
Language="VBScript"  "
in the beginning of the document to start of the script.

By experience, if you omit this part, while being on a IIS server, then it 
will work in ALL browser, including Netscape, Mozilla and Opera.

They dont tell you that when you start working on it. But this is due to 
the close relative relation between ASP and VBS that allows the code mostly 
to work as ASP in those cases.
This does require that you have ASP supported though, which is something I 
am still trying to find a way to do completely in this.

Further more, when it comes to the great little Norwegian browser, Opera, 
in your settings go and specify that the browser should identify itself as 
MSIE 6, then it should work.
