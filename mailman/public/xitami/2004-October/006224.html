Belarc Advisor can probably answer your hardware vs. software question.
http://www.belarc.com/free_download.html

I have been both a hardware & software guy since the late 70's & I learned with
a M$ OS it is best to look at software conflicts first & then look for hardware
problems.

Xitami will try to serve up all page requests & the only time it will cause a
problem is if it tries to serve more pages at a time than the OS can handle. 

If you are having crashes with just Xitami running make sure windows isn't
running something else in the background & decrease the number of connections
Xitami will serve.
