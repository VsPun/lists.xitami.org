The solution ended up testing the MTU setting on my connection. Using this
number, I changed the MTU setting in the router.

ping -f -l 1454 192.168.1.1

I dropped the mtu number until I got a successful ping reply.

Lo and behold, it is now available for all to see.

Thanks for everyone's suggestions.
