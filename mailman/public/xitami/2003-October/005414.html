Your request made me realize that perhaps Usenet might be a better
approach.  (1) it's distributed, rather than reliant upon a single server
site; (2) the propagation mechanisms are already there; (3) DejaGoogle
provides an archive and searching mechanism.

So I hunted around to see what sort of groups already exist; I had
expected to see, for example, groups discussing apache.  To my surprise,
althought there were some national groups (in japan.* and dk.*), and also
an alt group, there was nothing in the Big-8 comp.* hierarchy.

ISTM that the correct place would have been in a sub-division of
comp.infosystems.www.server.*; however, the taxonomy of that is by O/S
platform.  Is anyone on this mailing list already active anywhere within
that comp.i.w.s.* hierarchy?
