Indeed a very strange problem. I was trying to solve this problem for 10 
hours now.

I have developped quite a number of applications using Xitami, and for me 
it's the most easy method to implement a multi-user, multi-client 
application. Basically i know what is possible, and stick to it.
The last one is a complete POS (point-Of-Sales) application using touch 
screens and a full screen version of internet explorer.
Everything works fine (even with an outdated webserver).

The only problem was the part involving a real-time name lookup of a email 
address.
When the salespeople enter a e-mail address, it is required to have a email 
validation feature.
The normal way involves sending a automated e-mail to the customer, but 
when the salesperson receives the (negative) response, the customer has 
already leaved, and we have no way of contacting him.

I've tried out other webservers (Apache and Sambar), but Xitami is the most 
easy to configure.
btw Sambar has a very good documentation (but Xitami too)
