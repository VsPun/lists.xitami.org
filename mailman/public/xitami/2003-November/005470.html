I've seen a couple of  comments here concerning a potential problem with 
email addresses in the archive.  But no one has mentioned the 
registration requirements for forums.  In the case of the InReview site, 
reading their "Privacy Policy", shows you have no privacy from them or 
anyone else they want to give your information to.  Considering all of 
the pop-up ads they have, I'm sure my already over-burdened spam filter 
would be getting an additional workout, if I was foolish enough to 
register with them.  The ads are bad enough, but I understand that 
something has to pay their bills, but a blatant we can do anything we 
want with your information is totally unacceptable.

PS: Don't respond saying they have an opt-out provision, because we all 
know that by the time you opt-out the damage is already done, if it has 
any meaning at all.
