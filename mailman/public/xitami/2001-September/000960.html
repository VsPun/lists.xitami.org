Ben, 

In your defaults.cfg:

[Virtual-hosts]
    * = "basehost.cfg"
    www.benryder.co.uk = "foo.cfg"
    benryder.co.uk = "foo.cfg"

Where foo.cfg = your configuration file for www.benryder.co.uk

And no, you won't run out of lines to add virtual hosts in the .cfg file.
