I use BulkRegister.com.  If you are going to register a lot of domains, then
this is the way to go.  Their interface is easy and without all the NetSol
confusion.  their tech support is better also.

You pay an entry fee, which sucks, but if you register more than 10 domains
it pays itself back quick.

I pay as low as $8 to $10 dollars depending on my volume or their specials.
