Here is what works for me using 2.4d7 on W2K Pro (worked the same on NT 4.0
Server).  Our primary site is www.arbor-freight.com with a virtual site of
www.audctrl.com.
The default.cfg that I believe is automatically merged with the xitami.cfg
contains:
#*END
[Ftp]
    Email-check=1
    Root=t:\ftphome
[Security]
    Filename=defaults.aut
[Server]
    Cgi-bin=e:\webhome\cgi-bin
    Webpages=e:\webhome\arbor
[Virtual-hosts]
    www.audctrl.com=audctrl.cfg
Then there is an audctrl.cfg (as pointed to by the default.cfg) containing:
#*END
[Accesslog]
    Filename=audctrl.log
[Ftp]
    Root=t:\ftphome
[Security]
    Admin=0
    Filename=audctrl.aut
[Server]
    Cgi-bin=e:\webhome\cgi-bin
    Webpages=e:\webhome\audctrl
Note in our case we use the same ftp and cgi-bin for both the primary and
virtual, but use separate webpage directories.
Hope this helps!
