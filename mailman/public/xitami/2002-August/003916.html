Windows instruction then editing the php.ini file to point to an 
external mail server would work.

One thing about most mail servers, they don't allow open relays.  That 
means you have your machine log in and check your e-mail within 10 
minutes.  You mail server logs your IP for 10 minutes and them allows 
you to e-mail from that server for then next 10 minutes.

Try installing a small e-mail program that checks your e-mail 9 minutes.
