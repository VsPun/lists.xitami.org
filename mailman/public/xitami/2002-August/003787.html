Okay,

I'm an absolutelute rookie, but this works. Don't try anything else.

I opened Default.aut and saw this

[/Admin]
    jbailey="CurrentPassword"

I changed it to read
[/Admin]
    jbailey="NewPassword"

I terminated Xitami and restarted it. Then I went 127.0.0.1/admin, typed my
new password and presto, It was honored. If that is ALL you want to know,
this should make you happy.
