On Fri, 23 Aug 2002 10:26:59 -0400, WhiteShepherd wrote:

>In the past if Xitami crashed it would just reload and keep going.  After Xitami 2.5 is 
>installed when it crashes it brings up a PM window (The application has encountered a 
>problem and cannot continue. Press "OK").  Xitami will not reload untill you click the 
>"OK" button with your mouse.

in config.sys search & update or append this command(s):

AUTOFAIL=YES

will write the error into c:\popuplog.os2

depending on your warp version you may need to add

SUPPRESSPOPUPS=C (C is a drive letter)

also.

a good idea is to add 

REIPL=ON

also. instead of displaying the trap screen and hang the machine will
reboot now.

you have to reboot after you change the config.sys
