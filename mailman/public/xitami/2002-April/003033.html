Aliases is on the menu for the web browser admin screen. Just click Configuration and then on Aliases, which is on the right side.
Alias goes to Alias and Directory goes to Points to path. You should already have read access. To setup execute, just point the
cgi-bin to your alias or higher, like your web page directory.
