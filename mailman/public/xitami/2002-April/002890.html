Ervin,

No, I don't think he's referring to Frontpage. Just to HTTP's
'put' request. (See RFC 2068, under 9.6).

Xitami indeed states somewhere that they have no intention
to support Frontpage and gives as reason that it is not properly
documented and that they're not in the business of reverse
engineering Microsoft software.

So, since you claim FP to be a documented protocol, I would
be very interested in obtaining a link to that document!
Could you supply that to us?

Kind Regards,
Paul Claessen
