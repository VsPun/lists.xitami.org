> ftp server??? if you're on this list... don't you have xitami?<

Actually, I think a lot of us found the FTP service in Xitami to be...well,
not so stable.  Xitami is a great web server, that's what it is and that's
what it does best...FTP is something totally different and it only makes
sense to get a real FTP server that does...well, file transfers :)

> or do you mean ftp client? in that case - i recommend ws-ftp

Up until this last release (v7) I would have agreed with you, but Ipswich
really blew it with this one.  They've tried to put to much into a stable
product just for an upgrade and ended up with a 'feature-rich' program that
is very unstable.  Within the first 45 days of its release they had 9
updates....shades of MS.  To bad too, WSFTP was a great piece of work
before.
