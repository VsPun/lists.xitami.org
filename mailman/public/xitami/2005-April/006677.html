Ok this really worked good and simple once I analyzed what was going on.

In my site.cfg in this case miplanet.com I just added this under [Server]
remove the @error

text-404=test404.php

test404.php looks like this

<?php
$shorturl = 'http://www.miplanet.com/404.php';
header("Location: $shorturl");
?>

But it could be anything I want in this case I can keep my 404.php in my
site dir and redirect the 404 error to that file.

Sweet.
