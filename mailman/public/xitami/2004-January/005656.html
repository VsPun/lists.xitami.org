Hi Folks,

A quick administrive note: mail to the Xitami mailing list is now passed
through a spam scanner, and spam messages (scoring > 5 in SpamAssassin)
are silently discarded.  This is primarily to eliminate the spam
messages which arrive every day in the "message from non-list-member"
file approval.

I don't think this should interfere at all with normal messages to the
mailing list, but if anyone does encounter any problems then please let
me know directly (ewen@imatix.com).
