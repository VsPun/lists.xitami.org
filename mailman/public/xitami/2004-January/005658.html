from the outside (internet), people are  going to 24.209.120.13 which is
your router. you need to allow them through the router to your pc that has
the web server.

i always use NAT for this. do a search and you'll find tons of info. if your
router supports nat it will be in the help documentation for your router.

basically you have to translate port 80 (www) from your outside address
(24.209.120.13) to your inside address (192.168.1.2).
