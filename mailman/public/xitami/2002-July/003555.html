$REQUEST_URI is not a standard HTTP 1.1 enviromental varible. Its only 
used in Apache 1.21 and above(see http://www.apache.org).

Try $SCRIPT_NAME or $PATH_TRANSLATED in it's place. You might have more 
luck with these.
