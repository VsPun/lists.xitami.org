I use a hub and a router.

   ISP  ------- HUB ----- Router --- Internal PC's
                     |
                     |
                 Server
   
The gateway on my server points to the ISP
The gateway on the interal PC's points to the Router.
Even though the machines are in the same building, they 
are on completely different networks.

from internal PC's.
