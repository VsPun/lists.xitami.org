R O wrote, On 2008-01-08 21:14:
> I am getting into some rather new territory here.  My site is just about 
> 100% dynamic and I am beginning the process of converting long, hard to 
> read dynamic URLs to static URLs.  Static URLs,  I am learning, are much 
> friendlier to search engines and crawlers.
>  
> That being said, everything I see regarding the subject uses Apache 
> based modules....has anyone here done any dyanmic URL conversions with 
> perl on Xitami?

I don't think that's possible. You can make heavy use of aliasing, of 
course, to get somewhat prettier URLs but the apache (et al) stuff is 
being done with mod_rewrite (or equiv), and that isn't possible with 
Xitami AFAIK. One option would be to just use the 404 page for 
everything (don't laugh!), handling your own internal redirects, but if 
I am not mistaken Xitami doesn't allow scripting in error pages (my 
memory is a little sketchy here but I'm pretty sure I have tried such 
things).
