At 07:07 23/05/04, you wrote:
>Hello Listmembers
>
>My question is a bit off topic here in the xitami list, but still related 
>in a way to xitami as
>webserver. Webdesign is new to me as xitami is. To make things short: Does 
>someone know a way to
>disable downloading pictures which are shown on a webpage ?

Not possible, since the images must be downloaded before they can be 
displayed. And then, the user can look in his cache to find the right 
image. No fancy javascript "<onClick=alert...>" can't prevent this.

Possible solutions:
------------------
1
only display thumbnails and put the full size images in a restricted 
directory (only accessible with password)

2
use software to put your images in pieces and use the browser to display 
the whole image.
