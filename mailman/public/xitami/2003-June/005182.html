Hello !

It could be that the TCP/IP stack is not installed.
You need it, even for accessing local pages.

Goto start/settings/Control panel/Network and check if TCP/IP is installed.
If not, install: Add/protocol/Microsoft/TCPIP
Have your windows CD near you.
