At 02:16 PM 6/27/2001 -0400, you wrote:
>     I'm sure there's a way to look at who's been accessing my XITAMI FTP
>and HTTP servers but I can't figure it out.  Where do I look.  Is this
>information in a text file?  I think I looked through all the log files but
>didn't see anything of interest.
>     Thanks and take care.

Take a look at access.log, in the logs subdirectory of wherever you installed Xitami.  You might not be able to open it while Xitami is running, since it opens and locks that file when it starts up (at least for me; WinME, Xitami 2.4d9, YMMV).
If you (or anyone else) is interested, I've written a short perl script that goes through and analyzes this file.  http://xbones.resnet.gatech.edu/cgi-bin/logview.pl if you want to try it; email me if you want the source.
